# Building Scalable Systems

Microservices are not a silver bullet, but they solve specific scaling problems very well.

## The Monolith Trap

Starting with a monolith is usually the right choice. It simplifies deployment and testing.

## Breaking it Down

When your team grows and deployment cycles slow down, it might be time to extract services.
